Our Story

Born from the
Kenyan Spirit

Savanna & Spice was born from a deep love for Kenya's extraordinary culinary heritage. Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, we bring together the bold, honest flavours of our homeland — from the smoky nyama choma of the Maasai Mara to the fragrant coastal spices of Mombasa.

Our kitchen is a celebration of the diverse cultures that make Kenya extraordinary. We source every ingredient directly from local farms and markets, ensuring each dish arrives at your table vibrant, fresh, and full of character.

From a rooftop terrace with sweeping city views to intimate candlelit corners, Savanna & Spice is your space to eat, gather, and celebrate.

Chef David Mwangi
Executive Chef & Co-Founder
